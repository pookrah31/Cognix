How to Play

🧠 Reverse Recall

  • Memorize the sequence of numbers.
  • Enter them in reverse order.
  • Ignore floating shapes on Medium/Hard modes!

🎨 Color Clash

  • A word will appear (e.g., "RED").
  • Click the button matching the INK COLOR of the word.
  • Do NOT click the word it spells!

🔲 Pattern Matrix

  • A pattern of cells will flash on the grid.
  • Memorize the highlighted cells.
  • Click to reproduce the exact pattern from memory.

➕ Math Blitz

  • Solve arithmetic problems as fast as you can.
  • Pick the correct answer from 4 choices.
  • Speed bonuses for fast answers!

⌨️ Type Rush

  • Type the displayed words as fast and accurately as you can.
  • Press Space or Enter to submit each word.
  • Scored on WPM (words per minute) and accuracy.
